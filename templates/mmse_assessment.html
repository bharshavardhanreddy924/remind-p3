About This Assessment

The Mini-Mental State Examination (MMSE) is a comprehensive cognitive assessment tool that evaluates:

  • Orientation: Time and place awareness
  • Memory: Registration and recall abilities
  • Attention: Concentration and calculation skills
  • Language: Communication and comprehension
  • Visual-Spatial: Construction abilities
Assessment Process
